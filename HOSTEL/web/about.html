In the year 2002, Chitkara Educational Trust established its Punjab campus 30 kilometres from Chandigarh, on
the Chandigarh–Patiala National Highway. In the year 2010 Chitkara University was established by the Punjab
State Legislature under “The Chitkara University Act”.
Chitkara University, the best university in Punjab is a government-recognized university with the right to confer
degrees as per the Sections 2(f) and 22(1) of the UGC Act, 1956.
VISION
To be a globally recognized organization promoting academic excellence through interdisciplinary applied
research and to expand realms of knowledge through innovation.
